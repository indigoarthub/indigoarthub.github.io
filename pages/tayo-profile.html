Tayo Oladele

Osogbo, Osun State

With over 20 years of immersion in the indigo vats of Osogbo, Tayo has redefined the boundary between traditional Adire and contemporary fine art. His work is a visual record of Yoruba spirituality and the rhythmic pulse of the marketplace.

The Founder's Vision

Beyond the brush and the dye, Tayo serves as the technical architect of Indigo Art Hub. By manually coding this digital gateway, he ensures that the 15th-century heritage of Yoruba craft is protected by 21st-century innovation, creating a resilient bridge for the master artisans of Osogbo to reach the modern world.

"My mission is to ensure that the patterns taught to me by the elders don't just survive—they thrive in the modern home."
