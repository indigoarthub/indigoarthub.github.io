Preserving the Deep Blue.

The Art of Indigo

At Indigo Art Hub, we don't just sell art; we preserve a lineage. Our Batik process involves a meticulous wax-resist dyeing technique passed down through generations.

View Collection

The Mastery Process

Step 01

Hand-Drawn Wax

Intricate patterns are applied using hot wax to resist the deep indigo dye.

Step 02

Indigo Immersion

The fabric is dipped multiple times to achieve the iconic, soulful Osogbo blue.

Step 03

The Reveal

The wax is removed, revealing the stark contrast of white art against the indigo.

Common Inquiries

Do you ship internationally from Osogbo?

+

Yes! We partner with international couriers to ensure our Sacred Adire and Fine Art pieces reach collectors worldwide safely.

How do I care for my hand-dyed Adire?

+

To preserve the deep indigo soul, hand-wash in cold water with mild soap and avoid direct sunlight when drying.

Can I request a custom commission?

+

Absolutely. Our lead artisans accept private commissions. Please use the Contact page to start a conversation about your vision.
