# indigoarthub.github.io
🏺 Indigo Art Hub | A Loom & Logic Production
Heritage Craftsmanship. Deployed Globally.
Indigo Art Hub is the flagship digital gallery of the Loom & Logic agency. It is a high-performance, mobile-first architectural bridge designed to connect the master artisans of Osogbo, Nigeria, with the global art market.
By blending ancient Adire and Batik traditions with modern digital logic, we ensure that West African heritage isn't just preserved—it's premiered.
✨ Premium User Experience
We believe that high-end art deserves high-end performance. Recent upgrades include:
🏺 The Gold Preloader: A custom CSS/JS loading spinner that ensures a seamless entry into the gallery, providing immediate brand feedback while assets load.
🌊 Smooth-Scroll Navigation: Implementation of a global scroll-behavior for elegant, gliding transitions between gallery sections.
🖼️ Immersive Trio Design: A dynamic UI that shifts themes (Archive, Craft, Masters) to reflect the specific texture of Yoruba artistry.
✅ Official Favicon: The brand’s "Gold Pot" identity is fully integrated into the browser tab for a professional, polished finish.
🚀 Technical Architecture & Performance
Built with a "Zero-Bloat" philosophy using 3500+ lines of hand-coded, audited architecture.
Frontend: HTML5, CSS3 (Custom Variables/Grid), and Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+).
Optimized Assets: High-fidelity transparent PNGs and SVG-ready branding for sharp display on Retina/OLED screens.
Filtering Logic: Custom-built JavaScript gallery filters that allow collectors to sort through 13 rare masterpieces with zero page reloads.
Mobile-Coded: Architected specifically for the diverse connectivity environments of West Africa and the high-end demands of global collectors.
🛡️ The Collector’s Covenant
Trust is our core currency. The Hub includes a legally-audited "Collector's Covenant" to protect both the artisan and the buyer:
Safe Journeys: Intelligent exit-intent pop-ups to ensure collectors have all the information they need before leaving.
Transparency: Hard-coded Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and Refund Logic centered on the unique nature of handmade fine art.
